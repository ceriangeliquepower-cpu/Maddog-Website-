Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions.

Do I need any experience to start? +

Absolutely not. We welcome complete beginners across all our programmes. Our coaches will meet you where you are and build from there. Your first class is free — just show up.

What should I bring to my first class? +

Comfortable training clothes and a water bottle. For striking classes, hand wraps and gloves are ideal (we have loaners available). Just arrive 10 minutes early and introduce yourself.

How much does membership cost? +

Membership pricing varies depending on the programme and frequency. Contact us directly for current rates — we'll find a structure that works for you.

Do you have classes for kids? +

Yes. Our Junior Academy is open to ages 8–17 and covers the fundamentals of MMA, discipline, confidence, and respect. Contact us to enquire about current session times.

Is the gym open to women? +

Absolutely. Our head coach Amanda "Maddog" Lino is a 2× EFC World Champion and IMMAF World Champion. Maddog is built for everyone — female athletes are a core part of our community.

What disciplines do you offer at Maddog Performance Institute? +

We offer MMA, Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (Gi and No-Gi), Kickboxing, Boxing Fitness, Powerlifting, and Elite Personal Training. We also run a Junior Academy for ages 8–17, Corporate Wellness programmes, and a fully equipped Sports Recovery Suite with IV drip therapy, infrared sauna, and cold plunge.

Where exactly are you located in Ballito? +

We are at 22 Sandra Road, Balvista Centre, Ballito, KZN — upstairs in the Balvista Centre, above the Spar. Parking is available on site. We are easily accessible from Durban and all areas along the KZN North Coast.

Can I book recovery sessions without being a gym member? +

Yes. Our Recovery Suite — including IV drip therapy, infrared sauna, and cold plunge — is available to non-members as standalone sessions. Contact us to book or enquire about availability and pricing.
