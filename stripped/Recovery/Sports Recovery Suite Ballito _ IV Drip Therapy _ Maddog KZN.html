🔥 Deep Tissue Recovery & Detox

Infrared
Sauna.

Infrared sauna penetrates deeper into muscle tissue than traditional steam sauna, accelerating metabolic waste removal and reducing inflammation. An essential tool for sports recovery on the North Coast of KZN.

Regular use is associated with significant reductions in DOMS (delayed onset muscle soreness) and improved sleep quality. Infrared wavelengths heat the body from within, triggering a deeper physiological response than conventional heat.
  • Deeper muscle penetration than traditional steam sauna
  • Reduces DOMS — delayed onset muscle soreness
  • Accelerates metabolic waste removal
  • Improves cardiovascular function and sleep quality
  • Supports detoxification and skin health

🧊 Cold Water Immersion · Inflammation Control

Cold Plunge
Ice Bath.

Cold water immersion reduces acute inflammation, accelerates muscle repair, and improves circulation through vasoconstriction and vasodilation cycles. The cold plunge at Maddog is not a wellness trend. It is a training tool.

Triggers the release of norepinephrine — associated with improved mood, focus, and mental resilience. Regular cold exposure has been linked to improved nervous system regulation and enhanced recovery between training sessions.
  • Reduces acute inflammation post-training
  • Accelerates muscle repair through vascular cycling
  • Triggers norepinephrine release — mood and focus
  • Builds mental resilience and cold tolerance
  • Can be combined with sauna for contrast therapy
