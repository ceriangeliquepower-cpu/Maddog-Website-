🤼 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu · Gi & No-Gi

BJJ
In Ballito.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu at Maddog Performance Institute is one of the most effective grappling systems in the world. Built on the principle that technique and leverage allow a smaller person to control and submit a larger, stronger opponent — it is both a competitive sport and one of the most effective self-defence disciplines available.

Our BJJ programme covers guard work, passing, sweeps, submissions, and positional control in both Gi and No-Gi formats. Beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. Competitors are guided through WBJJ, national, regional, and MMA application preparation.
  • Gi and No-Gi classes — both formats available
  • Guard work, sweeps, passing and positional control
  • Submission offence and escape
  • Beginner through to competitive competitor tracks
  • MMA application — ground and pound, transitions

🦵 Stand-Up Striking · All Levels

Kickboxing
In Ballito.

Maddog's kickboxing programme is built on the real striking system tested inside professional MMA cages across Africa. This is not a cardio class dressed up with gloves. Our sessions develop genuine boxing technique, powerful kicks, footwork, timing, and defensive skills — coached by a team with real competitive experience.

Whether you want to compete, use it as your primary stand-up for MMA, or simply want one of the most effective full-body workouts on the KZN North Coast — this programme delivers. Burns serious calories, builds lean muscle, improves cardiovascular capacity, and develops genuine skills.
  • Boxing fundamentals — stance, guard, combination work
  • Kicks — roundhouse, teep, low kick, body kick technique
  • Footwork, timing and defensive head movement
  • Pad work and partner drilling
  • Sparring (optional) — structured and supervised

Powerlifting
In Ballito.

Powerlifting at Maddog Performance Institute is coached with the same precision and discipline that builds world champions. Our powerlifting programme is built around the three competition lifts — squat, bench press, and deadlift — and is structured to develop maximal strength, technical mastery, and competition-ready performance.

Whether you are stepping onto a platform for the first time or chasing a total PB, our coaches provide the technical expertise, periodised programming, and performance environment to take your lifting to the next level.
  • Squat, bench press and deadlift — technical coaching from the ground up
  • Periodised strength programming — peaking for competition or personal bests
  • Competition preparation and meet-day coaching
  • Accessory work for strength development and injury prevention
  • Nutrition guidance to support strength and body composition goals
